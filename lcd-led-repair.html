CRT TV

Cathode Ray Tube are slowly becoming a thing of the past after the arrival of Liquid Crystal Display which was more compact and less bulky than the CRT. The resolution provided by the LCD was way superior that the CRT could even imagine.

But still, a lot of people have those CRT televisions as their infotainment medium installed at their house. We have good experience in handling such kind of Tv sets.Day by day these are becoming tougher to repair as it is now becoming difficult to get their components in the open market.

LCD TV

Liquid Crystal Display comparatively has thin displays as compared to CRT. LCD displays can also be seen on computer screens. These are much better than CRT in terms of visibility and occupy very less space.

LCD became more relevant in the last decade. They provide much better picture quality and are most sought after. Having all such features comes at a price but it is worth it.LCD does not give many problems for 3 to 4 years but after that the capacitors and resistors become lossy.

This is not a big problem, sometimes the problem is very small and can be fixed by a person having some level of expertise. However, sometimes things go another way around. Don't worry we have your back. Feel free to call Sony Service Centre Mumbai and we will take care of the rest
