About Home theatre

Home theatre sounds interesting right?. Well having one of these is really worth the experience it provides. It is also known as surround sound system.

The acoustics is awesome. Watching movies, matches or listening music becomes a good time. The receiver is at the heart of a typical home theatre system.

The receiver sends the video on to your television and sends the audio to the decoder. The decoder sorts out the different sound channels from the video signal, and then sends the information to amplifiers for each sound-channel output.
